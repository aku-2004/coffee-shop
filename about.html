About Us

The long version of our story.

How It Started

We opened in 2023 with one small room, a second-hand espresso machine, and not much of a plan beyond wanting a place in Chandigarh where you could get a proper filter coffee without it being a whole production. My wife Anjali had been roasting beans at home for years as a hobby, and I was tired of my old job, so we just... did it.

The first few months were rough, honestly. We didn't know how much coffee to roast, we ran out of milk on our second day, and our nephew Rohan (who now helps on weekends) still teases us about the time we forgot to open on a Tuesday because we lost track of the date. But people kept coming back, and slowly it became what it is now.

We're still a two-and-a-half person operation, if you count Rohan as the half (he'd disagree). Nothing here is run by a big team or a franchise playbook — it's just us, trying to make good coffee and be a nice place to sit for a while.

Where Our Coffee Comes From

Our beans come from a small estate near Coorg in Karnataka that we've been buying from since year one. It's not a big commercial farm — just a family who grows shade-grown Arabica and sells it in small batches. We roast it ourselves in-house, a little darker than most cafes in Chandigarh prefer, because that's how we like it.

Our tea comes from a grower in Assam that a friend introduced us to. For milk and other ingredients, we try to buy local where we can — it's not always cheaper, but it tastes better and it matters to us.
