our story

We're Still Just Us

We started this little place because we love good coffee and didn't think Chandigarh had enough spots to just sit, relax, and have a proper filter coffee. It's still just us running the show — my wife does the roasting, I handle the counter, and our nephew helps out on weekends.

We buy our beans from a small estate near Coorg and our tea leaves from a grower we've known for years. Nothing fancy, just stuff we'd want to drink ourselves. Come say hi, we're usually here from morning till closing.

The House Filter Coffee
